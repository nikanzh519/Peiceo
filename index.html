Introduction

Culture shapes how we perceive and address mental health. This website explores the profound connection between cultural norms and mental health practices worldwide.

Culture & Mental Health

From stigmas to coping mechanisms, culture influences how individuals experience and respond to mental health challenges. Collectivist societies, for example, may emphasize community support, while individualistic cultures might promote self-reliance.
